Hotpot

Description

Lots of meat and vegetables cooked in spicy soup base before being dipped in thick and savoury sauce. Yum!!!

Ingredients

  • Hotpot base
  • Meat
  • Bok choy
  • Beef stomach
  • Pig intestine
  • Dipping sauce

Steps

  1. Put hotpot base in the pot and add water to boil
  2. Add meat and vegetables to your liking
  3. Cook and enjoy!
